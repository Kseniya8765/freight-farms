Features

Compact size

The most compact high-output modular farm there is.

The small physical footprint of the M unlocks a new world of possibilities, where people can grow in places only dreamed of before.

Whether in a back yard or back alley, bring the farm to you, wherever you are.

Explore more

Manageable output

The Greenery M is designed to fit into your life.

With its minimal labor needs (5 hrs / week) and it’s manageable output, the M is the perfect place to begin your farming journey.

The M can grow up to 300 lbs of plants per month. At this max output, you only need 6 neighbors to run a successful CSA model or 2 chefs (if selling to restaurants).

Or if you just wish to grow for your family, at your own pace, the M is perfectly suited for that too.

Engaging design

The M is designed to be perfectly suited for daily interaction, both publicly and privately.

With an added glass viewing window, accessible via the farm’s sliding door design, our farmers can show anyone around what’s growing inside. An illuminating look at the farm from the outside, leaving people inspired and in awe.

Vertical farming has never been more fun.

Grow what you want

From herbs to hemp, pumpkins to pineapple sage, you can grow whatever your green thumb desires in the Greenery M.

Explore the wide array of varietals and plants that are otherwise impossible to find, bringing new flavors, colors, and plants to your family and your community.

With our Farmhand software, closed-loop hydroponic system, and climate controlled environmental growing, you can grow great plants of all shapes and sizes, with consistency and ease.
